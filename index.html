Let me tell you abit about this fella!

Shawn Jonas Wolf is the second son of five boys in his family and the first to graduate with a college degree, currently he lives in Minneapolis, MN with his husband. They have lived and loved together for over 15 wonderful years in a nice little condo unit near downtown. Shawn has worked as an Mortgage Loan Underwriter, Operations and Credit Analyst for Wells Fargo Home Mortgage. Before that he worked as a Security Dispatcher while doing a short stent as a freelance comic book/fantasy artist.

Shawn has studied Mandarin Chinese as well as traveled to China, though not fluent he has a healthy appreciation for other cultures. With his Associates Degree in Software Development (specializing in C# and Visual Basic), he spends of his time perfecting his coding abilities while enrolled at the U of M Coding Boot Camp. Shawn also takes time out developing himself through physical training at Los Campeones Gym under Tyler Caldwell.

Shawn's favorite color is Orange followed by Candy-Apple Red. Shawn's favorite foods are burgers, and pizza, favorite drinks include coffee, Knob Creek Manhattan's and orange juice. His favorite poison besides life is taking cruises.
